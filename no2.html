By your own numbers, this is the single biggest week of your year, Pride and the World Cup and everything ramping at once. So this one is short on purpose. Three anchors, one growth edge, one piece of science, one joy. No overwhelm. Let's get you a great start.

1You · the growth edge

This week, your move is to receive.

When I stripped the story away and ranked your life on hard data, it all landed on one thing: across money and relationships, you give far more than you take. So your one personal practice this week is small, and for you, genuinely hard.

The moveWhen someone offers you something this week, help, a ticket, a hand, a thank-you, say yes and let it land. Don't deflect it, don't repay it on the spot. Receiving isn't weakness. For you it's the growth.
2People · who's carrying you

Let the people who love you do their part.

You don't have to hold this week up alone, and you're not meant to. Pete is bringing you to the match. Quinn is flying back to be there. Eric is already carrying the operational weight underneath everything. That's not you failing to do it all, that's the system working.

Your job this week is to be carried by the people who love you, and to let them feel good about it.

4AI · receiving, applied to me

Hand me what drains you.

Receiving help includes letting me carry the wide, shallow work, the triage, the drafts, the follow-ups, the logistics, the "can you find out." Save your energy for the rooms only you can be in. This week, when something feels like friction, the reflex is simple: send it to me first.

5Fog of war · one new idea

Allostatic load.

Here's the science nobody tells you. Stress isn't damaging because of any single event, it's allostatic load: the cumulative wear from peaks that never fully reset. Your body keeps the tab open. The fix is not toughness or willpower; it's recovery between the peaks, and letting others absorb some of the weight so the tab doesn't run.

This week is a textbook high-load week. The encouraging part: you don't lower the load by doing more. You lower it by planning the troughs, and by receiving. The science and your growth edge are the same lever.

6Joy · the reward

Two moments that are yours.

Thursday's match with Pete and Quinn, and Sunday's parade in Chicago. These aren't items on the list to get through, they're the point of the loud week. Be all the way inside both. Phone down. That's the receiving, too, receiving joy.

Your one move for a great start

Today, Monday.

Do the one thing only you can do today. Hand me or someone else the rest. Then text one person back a simple "yes, thank you." That's the whole week's practice, in one small act. You've got this, and you're not doing it alone.
